About Sage Partners

Commercial thinking, expressed through better design.

We do not treat a Shopify store like a collection of attractive sections. We treat it as a complete buying system: positioning, navigation, product discovery, persuasion, trust, speed and retention working together.

Since 2019, the team has supported ecommerce brands with Shopify development, redesign, technical optimization, SEO, acquisition and ongoing store operations.

Research before decoration

Every visual decision should support clarity, credibility or conversion.

Mobile is not an afterthought

Layout, content order and interactions are designed for smaller screens first.

Clean systems scale

Reusable components and maintainable code reduce future friction.

Strategy stays practical

Recommendations are prioritized by impact, effort and commercial urgency.

FAQ

Important questions, answered clearly.

A strong project starts with realistic expectations, transparent scope and a shared definition of success.

What does a typical project include?

Every engagement starts with scope definition and a commercial objective. Deliverables can include research, UX direction, design, development, QA, analytics, launch support and a clear handover.

Do you work with existing Shopify stores?

Yes. Most projects involve improving an existing store through redesign, technical fixes, conversion optimization, SEO, merchandising or ongoing management.

Can you work from a reference website?

Yes. References help clarify visual direction, but the final experience is adapted to your products, brand, audience and conversion requirements rather than copied.

How do you price projects?

Pricing depends on store size, complexity, research depth, integrations and delivery scope. After an initial review, you receive a clear proposal with inclusions and priorities.

Is the website optimized for mobile?

Yes. Mobile is treated as a primary buying environment, with deliberate spacing, typography, navigation, content order, image treatment and tap targets.

Can you support the store after launch?

Yes. Ongoing support can cover updates, merchandising, technical maintenance, analytics, optimization experiments, campaigns and growth planning.
